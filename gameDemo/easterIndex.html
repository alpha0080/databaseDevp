Game Description

Two towering moai statues welcome explorers to our Pacific paradise, where every win triggers a respin with the winning symbol replacing either all high or low pay symbols. Hit a win line with either 4 or 5 symbols and your reels will grow up to 4 rows taller! To top it off – the sticky wilds on reels 2 and 4 always expand to cover the whole reel. You can expect your players to extend their stay on this mirthful and mana-filled mystery island!
